LOGXLABS assesses your ecological footprint

Humanity is looking for alternative energies to fossil fuel combustion, firstly to reduce global warming and secondly to cope with oil shortage.

At LogXlabs, we think that it is urgent to reduce energy consumption, whatever its origin.

An immediate benefit will come from the reduction of the greenhouse effect - as the current primary energies still rely on fossil energy sources – but also from the control of energy requests that will allow new energies to represent a more significant part of the energy mix.

LogXlabs, being an optimisation specialist, ought to do something. Optimising starts by counting ;

The objective is to count all greenhouse gases (according to the Kyoto protocol) related to the activities of an entity in order to evaluate direct and indirect uses of energies, taking into account of :

  • Volumes of purchased oil, coal, gas,
  • Raw materials: metals, plastics, glass,
  • Energy consumption (kWh),
  • Transport: distances, transport modes, weight, number of people involved,
  • Manufactured goods purchased, quantity of paper, plastics and computer equipment,
  • Recycling cost.

This inventory allows us to identify the mass-producing emissions of greenhouse gases and to define the tactics to reduce them while respecting of social, economic, technical and financial constraints.

From this stage, our metaheuristics take over and provide solutions satisfying the constraints while maximising the goals.
